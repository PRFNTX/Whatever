Miracle of Sound

Gavin Dunne

Bio:

Gavin Dunne is the man behind the internet music project Miracle Of Sound. His songs and compositions (produced & mixed 100% solo from his home studio) cover a vast range of musical genres and styles and have accrued over 200 million views and streams across the internet through grassroots viral word of mouth and without help from record labels.

Industry giants such as Ubisoft, Bioware, EA, Epic and Bethesda have taken notice of the gaming sub-section of Gavin’s work and his songs have been used to promote major titles such as Mass Effect 3, Assassin’s Creed Syndicate and Watch Dogs.
